Welcome to the Rodgers Playbook Visual Library

This is a two-image teaching system designed for civil engineering onboarding. Every slide pairs two images so interns can see how drawings connect to real-world conditions.

  • Pick a topic module on the left (10 are preloaded)
  • Click any image slot to upload a photo or CAD export
  • Use the Narration panel to record or upload your voice
  • Fill in the 5-part Teaching structure so every slide is scaffolded the same way
  • Hit Present to run the slideshow fullscreen with narration auto-play
